How to use the sofa

  • Since the sofa is packaged, transported, and installed immediately after production, the initial leather (fabric) smell may be emitted
    and will begradually removed when ventilated.
  • During product transportation, there may be compression (armrest, head wrinkling) due to the weight between products, and the interior material
    is gradually restored and the pressed part is restored.
  • Please refrain from using a heating device or fire near the sofa as it may cause product deformation.
  • The color of the leather (fabric) may change when exposed to ultraviolet rays or direct sunlight.
  • If children run on the sofa or use more than the standard number of people, the frame may be damaged.
  • Users are responsible for product damage or accidents caused by customer negligence.
