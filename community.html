Frequently Asked Questions

Information on furniture delivery and shopping mall usage

  • Q. How do I cancel membership?

    A. Click the [Withdraw Membership] button at the bottom of the member information edit menu.

  • Q. I want to have it delivered on a specified date.

    A. In the metropolitan area, you can make a reservation for the specified date when you order at least 10 days in advance.
    However, it is not possible to specify a time, and it may be difficult to make a reservation for a specified date during a period of high volume of deliveries.
    If you leave the requested date in the delivery message when ordering, the person in charge will contact you after confirming the schedule.
    In some areas of the metropolitan area and other areas, delivery on the specified date is not possible because it is moved to a local logistics warehouse and delivered.
